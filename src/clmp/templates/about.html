About Us

Motivation for CLMP

The idea of a social contract has been at the heart of society since the early formations of cities and governments.

Whether explicit or implicit, these agreements have formed the basis for modern political and economic development.

Our Goal

Technological advancements in computing power and resource access has created opportunities for more decentralized organizations, and has opened the door for an innovation in contractual agreements.

Our goal will be to make contracting easy, seamless, and accessible to all individuals.
